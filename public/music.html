I've been making electronic music since 2012, as a lot of 13 yr olds do when they [[completely legally acquire]] FL studio, to make dubstep after hearing Skrillex for the first time in their lives. From there I branched out to D&B, particularly Liquid and Dancefloor.

It's not easy being one of the few people in the entirety of Norway who makes Drum and Bass, but through the sheer power of autism, I've managed to contribute a small portion to the liquid DnB scene. I found my footing just making music with people online and eventually released my first EP on Surreal Recordings.

Succeeding this, I've released with different core Liquid/Dancefloor DnB labels such as Subsphere, High Tea and Liquicity, with one notable release being a collaboration with Boxplot. Over the years I've also gained DJ support from Porter Robinson, Grafix (who's website I also designed btw), Feint and Nelver.
